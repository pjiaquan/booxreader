How it works

Resilient sync pipeline

  • Book files: On first open, the EPUB uploads to Firebase Storage with checksum metadata; repeated opens reuse the stored copy if unchanged.
  • Progress: Locator JSON cached locally and mirrored to Firestore for instant resume on any device.
  • Bookmarks & notes: Stored locally for offline, then pushed to Firestore; pulls on demand to keep devices aligned.

Privacy stance

Your library and notes are scoped to your Firebase user. No sharing or public listings—just your data on your devices.
